Welcome to the Tech-Ant Website

The Carrier-ant is a modular and electric all-terrain vehicle designed for defense operations. Its ability to adapt to various terrains and its eco-friendly technology make it a unique solution for modern military challenges.

Key Features

  • Modular design – adaptable for various missions
  • Electric powertrain – silent and eco-friendly
  • Remote controllability – ensuring safety and flexibility
  • Terrain-adaptive chassis – optimized for challenging conditions
