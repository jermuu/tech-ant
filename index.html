About Tech-ant Oy

Founded on 07.12.2021, Tech-ant Oy specializes in developing innovative military technologies that emphasize adaptability, efficiency, and localized production. Our primary mission is to deliver advanced solutions that meet the evolving needs of military forces worldwide, using cutting-edge 3D printing technologies and modular designs.

Our Projects

Tech-ant Oy is currently working on several military technology projects, including Carrier-ant, a modular military vehicle designed for harsh terrain and efficient localized production. All of our projects are built with the future of defense technology in mind, combining flexibility, innovation, and sustainability.
