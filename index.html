What is the Carrier-ant?

The Carrier-ant is an innovative, modular all-terrain vehicle specifically designed for modern defense needs. With cutting-edge technology, it combines adaptability, stealth, and eco-friendliness to meet the challenges of today's military operations.

Key Features

  • Modular Design: Adaptable for diverse missions, from transport to command operations.
  • Electric Powertrain: Quiet and efficient, reducing thermal emissions and increasing stealth capabilities.
  • Terrain-Adaptive Chassis: Optimized for challenging environments, ensuring superior mobility.
  • Remote Controllability: Allows for unmanned operations in dangerous environments.
