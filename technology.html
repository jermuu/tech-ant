Modular Design

The modular design allows Carrier-ant to be configured for a variety of mission types. The vehicle can be adapted for transportation, medical evacuation, or as a mobile command center.

Electric Powertrain

Carrier-ant operates with a fully electric powertrain, providing quiet, eco-friendly movement. This makes it ideal for stealth operations and helps reduce the carbon footprint.

Terrain-Adaptable Chassis

The terrain-adaptive chassis is designed to adjust automatically to varying ground conditions. Whether in rough, mountainous terrain or flat plains, Carrier-ant maintains stability and performance.

Remote Controllability

The vehicle can be operated remotely, ensuring the safety of personnel in hazardous environments. This feature also allows for autonomous or semi-autonomous missions, reducing the risk to soldiers.
